Toyota Company's History'

Toyota's history began with inventor Sakichi Toyoda's automatic looms, leading his son Kiichiro Toyoda to found Toyota Motor Co., Ltd. in 1937, evolving from a division of Toyoda Automatic Loom Works into a global automotive giant known for quality, reliability (like the Corolla), and innovation (like the Prius hybrid). The name changed from "Toyoda" to "Toyota" for luck (8 strokes in Japanese) and branding, marking a transition from weaving machinery to mass-producing cars and trucks for a growing world.
